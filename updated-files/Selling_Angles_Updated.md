**SELLING ANGLES & PITCH GUIDE**

AI Implementation Partner --- By Vertical and CRM Stack

*Updated March 2026 \| Internal Sales Reference*

**Universal Framing --- Lead With the Relationship**

Across all verticals and all CRM types, we no longer lead with features.
We lead with the relationship. The pitch always ends the same way:

> *\"You focus on running your business. We handle everything AI.\"*

Don\'t open with \"we capture emergency leads.\" Open with the partner
frame first, then prove it with the specific outcomes that matter for
their vertical.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Universal Pitch:** \"We become your AI operations partner. We build
  the systems, manage them, stay current with every new tool that comes
  out --- including things like NemoClaw launching right now --- and
  proactively bring you new ways to recover revenue and cut costs. You
  focus on running your business. We handle everything AI.\"

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**The Universal Offer**

Regardless of vertical or CRM, the offer framing is always the same:

> *\"30-day free trial. We set everything up. If we don\'t capture at
> least 5 leads and fill 2 cancellations in 30 days, you pay nothing.\"*

This removes all risk objections. The CRM type and vertical only
determines integration complexity on the backend --- the client doesn\'t
need to think about that.

**Discovery Framework --- Any Vertical**

Before pitching features, run this discovery sequence. The answers tell
you which angle to lead with.

**The 5 Discovery Questions**

-   \"When a customer calls or texts after hours --- what happens to
    that lead?\"

-   \"How often does a job cancel or a customer no-show in a given
    week?\"

-   \"Who handles the scheduling and dispatch right now --- and what
    does their day look like?\"

-   \"What tool do you currently use to manage jobs and customers?\"

-   \"If you could recover one type of revenue you\'re currently losing,
    what would it be?\"

Their answers determine your selling angle. A business bleeding on
after-hours leads gets the lead capture pitch first. A business drowning
in idle staff gets the cancellation recovery pitch first. Same product,
different entry point.

**HVAC Vertical Playbook**

Use this section to tailor your pitch based on which CRM an HVAC
contractor uses.

**ServiceTitan Contractors**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Sell to them?** Yes --- but carefully. Highest-value prospects.
  Already paying \$1,200+/month. Financially sophisticated enough for ROI
  conversations.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**Selling Angle**

> *\"ServiceTitan is your system of record. We\'re your system of
> action. ST tells you what happened --- we make things happen before
> you even open your laptop.\"*

**The Hook**

ServiceTitan has some AI features but they\'re basic and locked inside
their ecosystem. We add the autonomous response layer ST doesn\'t have:

-   2 AM lead capture with instant SMS response

-   Cancellation SMS recovery to fill idle slots

-   Proactive outreach and follow-up automation

-   A retained partner who watches the AI landscape and rolls out new
    capabilities before ST does

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **NemoClaw Angle for ST Clients:** ServiceTitan clients are large
  enough to have heard about NVIDIA\'s NemoClaw from GTC 2026. Use it:
  \'The enterprise AI agent wave is here. We\'re already building on top
  of it for HVAC businesses. You\'ll have agentic AI running for your
  operation before your competitors even figure out what it means.\'

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**Pricing**

\$2,000--\$2,500/month. These contractors are bigger --- more
technicians, more leads, more cancellations. The ROI math scales up with
them. They won\'t blink at \$2,500 if you show \$8,000+ in recovered
revenue.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Caveat:** Can\'t fully integrate ST until partner API access is
  approved. Interim play: sell the vision, onboard via Zapier/Make.com as
  a bridge, plug in the real API once approved. Apply for ST partner
  access now --- approval takes 4--8 weeks.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**Jobber Contractors**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Sell to them?** Yes --- this is your MVP customer. Smaller,
  scrappier, more open to new tools. Owner-operators who feel the pain of
  missed leads personally.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**Selling Angle**

> *\"Jobber handles your invoices and scheduling. We handle everything
> Jobber can\'t --- the 2 AM emergency call, the cancelled job slot, the
> dispatcher tasks eating your Saturday.\"*

**The Hook**

Jobber\'s automations are template-based and reactive. Ours are
intelligent and proactive:

-   NLP urgency classification --- Jobber has none

-   Detects cancellations and fills them via SMS outreach

-   Qualifies leads and responds in under 30 seconds, 24/7

-   A retained partner who adds new capabilities over time --- not a
    static tool

**Pricing**

\$1,500/month. Jobber contractors are price-sensitive. \$1,500 is the
sweet spot --- significant enough to take seriously, justifiable with
the ROI math.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Integration Advantage:** Full API access from day one. Fastest path
  to a live demo with real data. This is your first-mover platform.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**HouseCall Pro Contractors**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Sell to them?** Selectively. Sit between Jobber and ST in size.
  Popular with residential HVAC contractors who want something simpler
  than ServiceTitan.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**Selling Angle**

> *\"HouseCall Pro is great for running your day-to-day. But it\'s not
> watching your business at 2 AM, it\'s not texting customers when a job
> cancels, and it\'s not chasing unpaid invoices automatically. We
> are.\"*

**The Hook**

HCP users chose it because they wanted simple. Position your system as
the intelligence layer that makes their simple CRM punch above its
weight --- without switching platforms:

-   No migration, no retraining, no disruption

-   Same simplicity they love, now with autonomous revenue recovery

-   A retained partner they never have to train or manage

**Pricing**

\$1,200--\$1,500/month. Start at \$1,200 to reduce friction, upsell to
\$1,500 once you prove ROI in month one.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Caveat:** HCP\'s API is less mature. Be upfront that some features
  (deep calendar sync, real-time tech location) may have a short delay in
  availability.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**No CRM / Spreadsheet Contractors**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Sell to them?** No --- not yet. Without a CRM there\'s no data layer
  to sit on top of, no webhook to listen to, no job history to pull from.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**The Exception --- Full Stack Bundle**

If a no-CRM contractor is high-revenue (\$2M+) and clearly feels the
pain, offer a CRM onboarding + system bundle:

-   Help them get set up on Jobber

-   Layer your system on top immediately after

-   Charge \$3,000--\$4,000/month for the full package

-   High-touch but high-margin --- creates a sticky long-term retained
    relationship

**Other Verticals --- Universal Pitch Template**

For any non-HVAC vertical, use this pitch structure. Fill in the blanks
from your discovery call.

**Universal Selling Angle**

> *\"\[Their tool\] handles \[what it does\]. But it\'s not \[outcome
> 1\], it\'s not \[outcome 2\], and it\'s not \[outcome 3\]
> automatically. We are.\"*

**The Universal Hook**

-   Their current tool is reactive --- ours is proactive

-   Their current tool requires a human to act --- ours acts
    autonomously

-   Their current tool is a static subscription --- ours is a
    relationship that evolves

**Objection Handling**

**\"Our CRM/tool already does this.\"**

\"Great --- it captures the data. We do everything that happens next:
qualify the urgency, respond in 30 seconds, fill the cancellation that
opens up, chase the invoice, request the review. Your tool catches the
fish. We run the whole fishing operation.\"

**\"We can\'t afford another subscription.\"**

\"This isn\'t a subscription --- it\'s a retainer on a partner. And the
ROI math is simple: we need to recover \[X leads\] or fill \[Y slots\]
per month to pay for itself. Everything beyond that is margin. Most
clients see payback in 60--90 days.\"

**\"We\'re already looking at \[competitor tool\].\"**

\"Good --- use it. We\'re not a CRM, we\'re not replacing what you have.
We\'re the autonomous layer on top of it. Most of our clients use
multiple tools and we connect them all.\"
