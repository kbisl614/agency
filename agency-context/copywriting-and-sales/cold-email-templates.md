## Sequence Tips

**Timing:**
- Email 1: Day 0
- Email 2: Day 3–4 (if no reply)
- Email 3: Day 7–8 (final)

**Personalization (in order of impact):**
1. First name
2. Specific CRM (reference it — but frame as "layered on top of" not "only works with")
3. Specific pain they've mentioned publicly (Facebook group post, forum, etc.)
4. Their market/area if referencing exclusivity

**Response Handling:**
- "Not right now" → Follow up in 30 days
- Objection → Address directly (see Selling Angles doc)
- Interest → Move to discovery call within 24 hours
- No response after 3 emails → Archive, retry in 60 days

## What Works

- Lead with the specific problem, not the product
- Give specific dollar amounts ($300–800 per job, 4–6 cancellations/month, $3,900/month in missed calls)
- Keep emails under 150 words
- CTA is always "15-minute call" — never "sign up" or "buy now"
- Reference their specific CRM — generic outreach gets ignored
- One clear angle per email — never list features
- Custom framing every time — "built for your operation," not "our platform"
- Exclusivity signal ("one contractor per market area") creates urgency without pressure

## What Doesn't Work

❌ "AI-powered" or "revolutionary" — ignored
❌ Feature lists — nobody reads them
❌ Generic outreach not referencing their CRM
❌ Long emails that require scrolling
❌ Guarantee language — removed, don't use it
❌ "No pitch" — inaccurate, don't use it
❌ Technical language (n8n, webhook, Supabase, API)
❌ Vague subject lines
❌ Implying we only work with specific CRMs
❌ Named agents (Concierge, Closer, Dispatcher, Strategist) — use "agent(s)"
