## Sequence Tips

**Timing:**
- Email 1: Day 0
- Email 2: Day 3-4 (if no reply)
- Email 3: Day 7-8 (final)

**Personalization (in order of impact):**
1. First name
2. Specific CRM (Jobber/ServiceTitan)
3. Specific pain they've mentioned publicly (Facebook group post, etc.)
4. Their market/area if referencing exclusivity

**Response Handling:**
- "Not right now" → Follow up in 30 days
- Objection → Address directly (see Selling Angles doc)
- Interest → Move to discovery call within 24 hours
- No response after 3 emails → Archive, retry in 60 days

---

## What Works (Keep These)

- Lead with the specific problem, not the product
- Give specific dollar amounts ($300-800 per job, 4-6 cancellations/month)
- Guarantee early — removes the risk objection before they raise it
- Keep emails under 150 words
- CTA is always "15-minute call" not "sign up" or "buy now"
- Reference their specific CRM
- One clear angle per email — don't list features

## What Doesn't Work (Avoid)

❌ "AI-powered" or "revolutionary" — ignored
❌ Feature lists — nobody reads them
❌ Generic outreach not referencing their CRM
❌ Long emails that require scrolling
❌ "30-day free trial" — we don't offer this
❌ Technical language (n8n, webhook, Supabase)
❌ Vague subject lines
